Calgary Winter Market Update: Why Now Might Be a Great Time to Buy a Home

As we move into the heart of fall and approach the winter season, many assume the real estate market goes into hibernation. But here in Calgary, the data tells a different story — and if you’ve been waiting for the right time to step into the market, this winter could be your golden window of opportunity.

What's Happening in the Calgary Real Estate Market?

The Calgary housing market is starting to shift in ways that benefit buyers more than we’ve seen in quite some time. Here are some key developments to keep in mind:

  • ►More Homes on the Market:
    As of late September 2025, active listings across the city have increased by over 36% year-over-year, with inventory levels nearing 6,900 properties. This is a significant jump, and it means more selection for buyers — whether you’re looking for a detached home in the suburbs or a downtown condo.
  • ►A Shift Toward a Balanced Market:
    Months of supply — a key indicator of market balance — is now sitting at approximately 4.0 months, up from just 2.5 this time last year. This suggests we’re slowly moving away from the red-hot seller’s market of previous years and entering a more balanced or even buyer-favoring environment.
  • ►Prices Stabilizing or Softening:
    The benchmark price for residential properties in Calgary is around $572,800, while the median price has dipped slightly to approximately $560,000. We're not seeing major price drops, but the aggressive price increases of the last two years are definitely cooling. Buyers now have more room to negotiate — something we haven’t seen in a while.
  • ►Less Buyer Competition:
    The sales-to-new-listings ratio has dropped to around 45%, which tells us that homes are no longer flying off the market as quickly. This also means you’re less likely to face bidding wars or rushed decisions.
  • ►Homes Sitting Longer = More Negotiation Power:
    The average days on market (DOM) is climbing, giving buyers more breathing room to make thoughtful decisions. Sellers are more open to discussing price reductions, flexible conditions, or even contributing to closing costs.

Mortgage Rates Are Easing — Time to Lock One In

In addition to the shift in the housing market itself, mortgage rates are also creating a timely opportunity. After several years of increases, we’re now seeing some relief:

  • ►5-year fixed rates are currently available in the 3.9% – 4.2% range, depending on the lender and your financial profile.
  • ►Variable rates and 3-year fixed terms are also seeing competitive reductions.

If rates continue to fall, pre-approval becomes even more valuable — but even at today’s levels, locking in a pre-approval now could save you thousands over the life of your mortgage. Many lenders offer a 90–120 day rate hold, so acting now protects you if rates tick upward again.

Why Getting Pre-Approved Now Makes Sense

Whether you’re actively house hunting or just starting to explore the idea of buying, getting pre-approved gives you several major advantages:

  • ►Know What You Can Afford – No guesswork or surprises later.
  • ►Shop with Confidence – Sellers take your offer seriously when you’re backed by a lender.
  • ►Lock in a Great Rate – Secure today’s rate even if you buy a few months from now.
  • ►Be Ready When the Right Home Appears – In a shifting market, timing is everything.

Final Thoughts

The combination of increasing inventory, stabilizing prices, softer mortgage rates, and reduced competition makes this winter a window of opportunity for smart, prepared buyers in Calgary.
