Understanding Closing Costs

Beyond your down payment, it's essential to budget for closing costs, typically 1.5% of the purchase price. These may include:

  • Legal costs (approx. $3,000)
  • Title insurance and registration costs (approx. $400)
  • Appraisal costs (approx. $400)
  • Home inspection (approx. $500)
  • Moving costs

If you are purchasing a home please keep in mind it is very important to not purchase a home unconditional. Always place a finance condition in your purchase agreement.
